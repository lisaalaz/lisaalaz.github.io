Lisa Alazraki

Hi! I am a third year PhD student in the NLP Group at Imperial College London, supervised by Marek Rei. I am broadly interested in the relationship between reasoning and language. I also have an interest in model robustness to input perturbations and adversarial attacks.

Currently, I am affiliated with the Alan Turing Institute as a recipient of the Enrichment Scheme Placement Award. Previously, I was a Research Intern at Cohere (2024) and Google (2022, 2023).
