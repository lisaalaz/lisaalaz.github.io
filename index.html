Lisa Alazraki

Hi! I am a third year PhD student in the NLP Group at Imperial College London, advised by Marek Rei. I am broadly interested in the relationship between reasoning and language. I also have an interest in model robustness.

Currently, I am a Research Scientist Intern in the Llama Reasoning and Planning team at Meta GenAI, where I am working with Akhil Mathur. Previously, I was a Research Intern at Cohere (2024) and Google (2022, 2023).
